# 🫧 Bubble To-do

A playful little to-do widget: an illustrated girl peeks over the edge of your
screen. Click her and she pops out, blowing watercolor bubbles you write your
to-dos in. Double-click to puff a bubble off to the corner; click a floating
bubble to pop it done — with soft, synthesized sound effects.

### ▶️ Play it in your browser
**https://lingkan-wang.github.io/bubble-todo/**

### 🖥️ Desktop app (macOS, Apple Silicon)
A floating desktop sticky note — she lives on your screen edge.
**[Download the latest release »](https://github.com/lingkan-wang/bubble-todo/releases/latest)**
*(unsigned: first launch → right-click → Open)*

## What it does
- **Peek → pop → retract**: she rests as a peeking head; click to pop out, click empty space to tuck her back.
- **Write a to-do** in the bubble at her straw; **double-click her** to blow it into the corner.
- **Pop to complete**: click a floating bubble and it bursts (rupture ring + droplet spray).
- Idle **breathing** + a gentle bubble pulse; drag her anywhere.
- Sounds: a soft breath on blow, a crisp "pip" on pop — all synthesized, no audio files.

## Built with
Vanilla **HTML / CSS / JS**, zero dependencies. Animation via CSS + the Web
Animations API; sound via the Web Audio API. The desktop version is Electron.
